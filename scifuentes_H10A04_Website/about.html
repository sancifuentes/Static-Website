About us

Located in Montreal, Quebec, CARistic's main purpose is to buy unwanted cars, make the necessary repairs to make the car showroom quality, and then sell it to the public. Our cars range from hypercars, sport cars, SUVs, coupes, wagons, convertibles, and many more!
