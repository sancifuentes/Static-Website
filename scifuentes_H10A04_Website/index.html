CARistic provides the finest and most high-quality used cars in the market. Based in Montreal, Quebec, CARistic is owned by Muhammad Yasser Zafarullah and managed by Hector Martinez Do Santos. Looking for something to take to track day or maybe just a daily driver? No problem! Here at CARistic, our main goal is to offer only but the best cars to the public.

Our extremely talented staff is entirely devoted to CARistic's ultimate goal, to provide nothing but the best to the customers. We have a flexible schedule and offer unbeatable prices for our vehicles. You can read all of what a few of our satisfied customers have to say.
